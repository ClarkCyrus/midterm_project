VALORANT
VALORANT IS A FREE-TO-PLAY TACTICAL SHOOTER GAME DEVELOPED AND PUBLISHED BY RIOT GAMES. IT FEATURES A 5V5 MULTIPLAYER FORMAT WHERE PLAYERS ASSUME THE ROLES OF "AGENTS," EACH WITH UNIQUE ABILITIES THAT COMPLEMENT THEIR PLAYSTYLE. THE GAME COMBINES PRECISE GUNPLAY MECHANICS SIMILAR TO COUNTER-STRIKE WITH CHARACTER ABILITIES REMINISCENT OF HERO SHOOTERS LIKE OVERWATCH. VALORANT'S GAMEPLAY EMPHASIZES STRATEGY, TEAMWORK, AND PRECISE AIMING SKILLS. THE GAME HAS A COMPETITIVE RANKED MODE, AS WELL AS REGULAR UPDATES WITH NEW AGENTS, MAPS, AND GAME MODES TO KEEP THE EXPERIENCE FRESH FOR PLAYERS.

LEAGUE OF LEGENDS
LEAGUE OF LEGENDS (LOL) IS A MULTIPLAYER ONLINE BATTLE ARENA (MOBA) GAME DEVELOPED AND PUBLISHED BY RIOT GAMES. IN LOL, PLAYERS ASSUME THE ROLE OF A "CHAMPION," EACH WITH UNIQUE ABILITIES, AND COMPETE IN TEAMS TO DESTROY THE ENEMY TEAM'S NEXUS, A STRUCTURE LOCATED IN THEIR BASE. THE GAME COMBINES ELEMENTS OF STRATEGY, ROLE-PLAYING, AND TEAMWORK, REQUIRING PLAYERS TO COORDINATE WITH THEIR TEAM TO ACHIEVE VICTORY. LOL HAS A MASSIVE PLAYER BASE AND A PROFESSIONAL ESPORTS SCENE WITH REGIONAL AND INTERNATIONAL TOURNAMENTS, MAKING IT ONE OF THE MOST POPULAR AND INFLUENTIAL GAMES IN THE ESPORTS INDUSTRY.

MOBILE LEGENDS
MOBILE LEGENDS: BANG BANG IS A MOBILE MOBA GAME DEVELOPED AND PUBLISHED BY MOONTON. IT FOLLOWS A SIMILAR FORMAT TO LEAGUE OF LEGENDS, WITH PLAYERS SELECTING A HERO WITH UNIQUE ABILITIES AND BATTLING IN TEAMS TO DESTROY THE ENEMY TEAM'S BASE. MOBILE LEGENDS HAS SIMPLIFIED GAMEPLAY MECHANICS COMPARED TO TRADITIONAL PC MOBAS, MAKING IT MORE ACCESSIBLE TO MOBILE GAMERS. THE GAME HAS GAINED IMMENSE POPULARITY, PARTICULARLY IN SOUTHEAST ASIA, AND HAS A COMPETITIVE ESPORTS SCENE WITH REGULAR TOURNAMENTS AND A LARGE PLAYER BASE.
